Cinnamon Rolls

The recipe and how to make them

This recipe has been tested numerous times and it works even without accurate measurements!

For the rolls:

  • 3 eggs
  • 200ml or 1 cup of yogurt
  • 200ml or 1 cup of milk
  • 6 table spoons of sugar
  • 1 table spoon overfilled with active dry yeast
  • 600gr of flour or until you feel like the dough is strong enough
  • 6-8 cubes of butter around 1.5cm

For the filling:

  • 250gr of butter (minus the cubes used for the dough)
  • cinnamon enough to cover the sheet of dough
  • sugar enough to cover the sheet of dough

For the glaze: no measurements whatever your heart feels like

  • cream cheese
  • confectioners sugar
  • a bit of cinnamon

How to mix it all up

For the dough:

  1. Take the butter out of the fridge to let it get to room temperature so that it's spreadable.
  2. Mix the eggs with the sugar until they foam up a little then add the milk and yougurt, mix until they're all combined.
  3. When all the liquids are mixed up add in the yeast mix well and then add the flour and mix it with a wooden spoon until the dough comes together and is strong enough to knead.
  4. when the dough is srong enough you take it out of the bowl in a clean, flat, dusted with flour surface and start kneading. Start adding the butter to the dough one cube at a time and knead until the butter is well incorporated and put it back in the bowl.
  5. It's time to wait for the dough to rise double in size, it may take 1-2hours depending on the room temperature
  6. take the dough out of the bowl
